Talents

EMC offers solutions catered to the individual needs of every talent.

We work with a select number of talents around the year with a focus on building their fashion profile through elevated editorials.

Alexandra Pereira
The personable and elegant Alexandra Pereira joined EMC in the Fall of 2020 and is off to a powerful start, building relationships with some of the most desired fashion houses including Dior, Balmain, Chloé, Hermès, Miu Miu, Fendi and Louis Vuitton.

alexandrapereira

Brittany Xavier
Brittany Xavier has been part of the EMC Community since 2017. In record time, Brittany transformed into a Fashion It Girl who works regularly with Marc Jacobs, Dior, Louis Vuitton, Fendi, Versace, Chanel and many more.

brittanyxavier

Chriselle Lim
Chriselle Lim was the first influencer to sign on with EMC back in 2016 and the journey has been one that has taken Chriselle to the front row and around the world.

chrisellelim

Coco Bassey
Coco Bassey started off seasonally with EMC back in 2019, but when confinement hit, we stepped it up to year-round and have since doubled down on Coco’s fashion editorials working with brands including Salvatore Ferragamo, Fendi, Chanel and Tod’s.

cocobassey

Madelynn Furlong
Madelynn Furlong’s Parisian aesthetic was quickly embraced by the European fashion houses and has since transitioned into a unique voice that unites elevated fashion and design, resonating particularly well with Salone and Art Basel Miami.

madelynnfurlong

Leonie Hanne
Leonie Hanne came on board in 2018 with a flare for fashion and has since made it her creed, creating exceptional content on her various social media channels and the Media Impact Value to match.

leoniehanne

Sai de Silva
After a successful Paris Fashion Week, Sai de Silva is the latest talent to join the EMC’s year-round roster. The fierce and glamourous Brooklyn Mom has already caught the eye of Jean-Paul Gaultier Alaïa, Dior and Balmain and we’re just getting started.

scoutthecity

Alex Acero
Alex Acero is the first male influencer to join EMC year-round roster after having worked the shows together for over a year. He brings his gender bending aesthetic has caught the eye of luxury houses from renowned (Prada, Dior, Stella McCartney) to niche (Yohji Yamamoto, Kolor, WooYoungMi)

alexacerov
