Our story

After 10 years in the Hearst Magazine’s Paris office, Los Angeles born Emilie Meinadier took her invaluable network and expertise to set up her own agency, Emilie Meinadier Consulting, in 2007. Debuting with the Hearst portfolio, her client list grew rapidly to include the New York Times and T Magazine, W Magazine, Glamour (US), New York Magazine and GQ (US). With her distinct understanding of the demands of the American press, EMC synthesizes a unique palette of services centered around the European Ready to Wear and Haute Couture shows. Based in Paris, indisputably the premier fashion capital, EMC also covers Milan and London. In 2015, reflecting new evolutions in the industry, the agency’s first digital client, Instagram, came on board and, soon after, India Leclery joined the team to head EMC’s digital expansion.

European fashion weeks

Based on inside-out knowledge of the industry and of the European fashion capitals, we master logistics for the Men’s and Women’s Ready to Wear and Haute Couture shows in London, Milan and Paris.

  • on-site facilitator & executor for all Fashion Week operations
  • request, negotiate and secure invitations to shows, presentations & events
  • schedule strategic market and business meetings
  • arrange designer interviews & photo coverage
  • year-round development of relationships within the fashion and luxury industries
  • news on local cultural events

Digital services

We bring the same expertise to our portfolio of carefully selected digital influencers, during Fashion Weeks, and in the context of year-round engagements which facilitate organic brand positioning in luxury markets even further.

  • create concise and strategic promotional support to introduce the talent to our Fashion PR network through relevant imagery and text
  • send post fashion week reporting to the brands covered by the talent
  • build strategic relationships with fashion and luxury brands in the interest of harvesting future partnerships
  • image building and creative content consulting
