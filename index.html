C is for Consulting

Press

EMC started in 2007 as a life-handler for all Fashion Week operations for the American press. Emilie Meinadier debuted with her Hearst portfolio, then her client list grew rapidly to include the New York Times and T Magazine, W Magazine, several Conde Nast books and New York Magazine. The teams knew they could rely on EMC to organize a full mastered agenda throughout the shows. With her distinct understanding of the demands of the American press, EMC synthesizes a unique palette of services centered around the European Ready to Wear and Haute Couture shows.

Influence

In 2015, reflecting new evolutions in the industry, the agency’s first digital client, Instagram, came on board. Soon after, EMC embraced the influencer phenomenon starting with the then little known Chriselle Lim, accompanying her for her first Couture week. The agency began to support influencers not just during fashion week but also throughout the year, building loyalty between brands and talents. A front row seat at a show became the means, not the goal. Fast forward to five year later Chriselle Lim is one of the biggest fashion influencers and the EMC roster included over thirty talents.

Digital

In a constant changing world, EMC is always adapting to the meet the demands of the market. We have recently started accompany luxury brands as they navigate the social media landscape, creating meaningful influencer market strategies and experiences.

Services

Fashion Week

With a privileged perspective of the industry and the European fashion capitals, we master logistics for the Men’s and Women’s Ready to Wear and Haute Couture shows in New York, London, Milan and Paris.

  • On-site facilitator & executor for all Fashion Week operations
  • Request, negotiate and secure invitations to shows, presentations & events
  • Schedule strategic market and business meetings
  • Arrange designer interviews & photo coverage
  • Year-round development of relationships within the fashion and luxury industries
  • news on local cultural events and happenings

The EMC Fashion Week formula has been adapted to accompany clients anywhere fashion can be found including the Cannes Film Festival, Art Basel Miami and Milan Salone.

Brand Strategy

EMC brings their privy command of Fashion Weeks to digital influencers who look to gain recognition in the most exclusive fashion circles. Throughout the year, we advise talent them on their brand image, championing elevated pro bono content. Strategist, pioneer, insider, EMC gives every trick to speak fluent luxury.

  • Expertise in image building and creative content consulting
  • Build strategic relationships with fashion and luxury brands
  • Create powerful promotional support to introduce the talent to the EMC network
  • Circulate the talent’s profile through both specific outreach and broader newsletters
  • Provide artistic direction and styling inspiration through mood boards that allow the talent to identify to the brand’s unique realm
  • Reinforce the talent’s media value by communicating insights and impressions
  • Connecting talents and fashion PR

Team

Emilie Meinadier

A Los Angeles native, Emilie arrived in Paris in the early 1990s as a student and it was love at first site. Bright eyed and filled with determination, Emilie started building her network which incidentally allowed her to land her first job, working for the US editions of Harper’s Bazaar, Town & County & Cosmopolitan in the Hearst Magazine satellite office. Her ten years with Hearst took her to the most confidential corners of Paris and on fashion shoots around the world. When Hearst decide to close their offices, she started freelancing with the Hearst portfolio and soon after EMC was born.

25+ years later, Emilie is a true Francophile, the original Emily in Paris. She has a passion for travel and is always looking to discover the next culinary, cultural or fashion find, no matter the destination
